The rotation we’ve been waiting for is here

CPI printed soft on Tuesday — 2.6% year over year against 2.8% consensus — and the market did what soft prints do at this stage of the cycle: it sold dollars, bought duration, and reached for risk. The S&P barely moved. Underneath it, the whole crypto complex moved together.

“BTC dominance loses 52.4 and TOTAL3 breaks $685B in the same week. That has happened nine times since 2017, and on eight of them, alts ran for at least six weeks.”

The FOMC minutes confirmed what the press conference hinted at: three participants flagged that the case for a June cut had strengthened. Two-year yields broke their range to the downside. Real yields followed. Liquidity is opening, the dial nudged up to +3.4, and the indicator pulse turned all four green for the first time since November.

The discipline now is patience on entries and resolve on what we already own. The book is positioned. Let it work.
